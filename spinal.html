Flow for spinal mobilization

Perform each for a 15 second holds

  1. Head tilt ear to shoulder
  2. look left and right
  3. chin to chest
  4. look up
  5. standing fingers to lat knee
  6. standing sidebend full
  7. elbows to tummy
  8. elbows to sky
  9. standing twist
  10. Supine knee to chest
  11. supine rotation
  12. prone extension
  13. prone scorpion
